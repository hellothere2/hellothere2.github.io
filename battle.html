Note: Please enter 0 instead of simply deleting any values or else you will break the automator, giving you a bunch of "NaN"s in your results How it works:
Page detects unit name, type, and number of soldiers in unit. Each unit picks a target unit in enemy army to deal dmg to.
Each type has many stats: name, category, unitClass, soldiers, weapon, attack, armourPiercing, cavBonus, rangedWeapon, ammo, rangedPiercing, armour, defenceSkill, shield, and training.
The basics of it is this: Every single soldier in a unit rolls a dice vs defender dice, and if the attacker's dice is higher, then the defending unit takes a casualty
The attacking roll is d(attack) + training. Peasants have an attack of 4 and a training of 0, so they just get a d4 with no mods.
The defending roll is d(defenseSkil + armour + shield) + training.
If the attacker has armour piercing, the defender does not get armour as part of their roll.
If the attacker is a spearmen (like a town militia) and has a cav bonus, they add this bonus to their attack when facing a mounted unit, and so on.
Ranged units roll just like footmen, but instead attack x times, with x being equal to their ammo value. However, they are usually more vulnerable.
Cavalry roll just like footmen when facing other cavalry, but if heavy cav faces anything else they get massive bonuses.
In addition to a regular round of combat, they get a "charge" round of combat in which the enemy does not get any value out of shields and armour.
This represents the impact of a cavalry charge. If the infantry don't move out of the way, they will be trampled and killed by the horses.
