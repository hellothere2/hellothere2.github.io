Usage Instructions

Enter the relevant DUEL information in the appropriate boxes. If you do not specify values,
the program will automatically fill them in, and you might not like the results...
Hit "Submit Duel Values" to generate a full DUEL. You can copy and paste the results :)

Notes

Default HP value is 50, default skill bonus is 0. Duels function by rolling a d75 + Skill for each player (d50 if blunt weapons).
The difference between the attack rolls is taken from the lower roll's HP pool.
This occurs until one player reaches the "HP to stop duel at" number OR LOWER.
This means that you can still die in a friendly duel, since the other duelist could roll high
and take away health past the target HP.
When you click the Submit button, the whole page will be erased exept for the text involving the duel.
To return back, simply refresh the page when you are done.
