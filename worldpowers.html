SMP First-Past The Post Election Automator

Single Member Plurality (SMP), also known as First-Past-The-Post is a Majoritarian electoral system used in a small number of elections including the UK's House of Commons, the USA, Canada, and India.
The country is divided into single-member constituencies, usually of equal size. These are usually referred to as ridings or regions. There is only a single member attributed to this constituency (riding), therefore allowing easy translation of constituencies to seats in the country's legislature. Voters select a single candidate, usually marking his or her name with a X on the ballot. To win, a candidate only needs to achieve more votes than everyone else.

Usage Instructions

Enter the relevant election information in the appropriate boxes. This script will only generate stats for the winner of the election. You should base the seats of the other parties on the rolls displayed in the Results section.
Hit "Generate" to generate a First-Past the Post election. You can copy and paste the results :)
