Movement Info

Usage Instructions: Use a mouse pointer to highlight a hex. The hex you hover over will be indicated by a floating coordinates box along with a red highlight for clarification.
Clicking a hex will mark it purple and add it to the record of selected hexes. Once you're finished selecting hexes, click Copy Hexes to copy the Hex coordinates.
You can let another person see what hexes you selected by pasting into a message. The other user can copy your hex codes and paste them into the textbox.
Once finished, they should hit Load Hexes from Textbox. All of the hexes will be selected on their screen, and an alert will be given if the movement is invalid.
There is no way to clear hexes, so entirely new selections or loading someone else's selection should be done by first refreshing the page and then proceeding through the steps.
Clicking either of the buttons should be the last step performed on the page before you leave or refresh.
