Authority
Authority is legitimate power. Where power is the ability to influence others, Authority is the right. Authority is based on an acknowledged duty to obey rather than on any form of coercion or manipulation. Weber distinguishes three types of Authority: Traditional authority is based on history, Charismatic authority is based on personality, and Legal-Rational authority is based on a set of universal rules.
