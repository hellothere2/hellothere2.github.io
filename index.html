About

PerfectPigeons, located in Belgium, is the world's best pigeon breeding service. PerfectPigeons has been providing high-quality pigeons since 1647, with noteable clients such as Pope Francis (2013), House Medici circa 1700, and Pope Benedict XIV (1740-1758). Our business offers the world's finest pigeons, doves, and grooming services to some of the world's most influential people. We are able to handle extremely large transactions of several millions of dollars all over the world, so that you can trust where your money is going from the comfort of your home. Whether you are looking for a racing pigeon to win the world championship, a showbird worthy of a magazine cover, or a grooming service that you can trust, PerfectPigeons has got you covered. PerfectPigeons aims to offer our clients nothing less than perfect pigeons.

7K Duel/Joust Bot

This is the oldest service on the website, but still reliable. This page contains a tool that will automate duels and jousts using the mechanics detailed on r/SevenKingdoms. Accurate results guaranteed in less than one second.

7K Battle Calculator

Another old entry, this was built as an automator to help the SevenKingdoms mods quickly balance combat mechanics during development. The script was originally hooked up to run several million iterations from the same data, but was altered to fit player needs instead. This allows players to estimate the results of potential conflicts to better strategize.
